这是一个典型的优化示例：

SQL
SELECT u.name, SUM(o.amount) AS total_sales
FROM users u
JOIN orders o ON u.id = o.user_id
WHERE o.created_at >= '2023-01-01'
GROUP BY u.id
HAVING total_sales > 1000;
